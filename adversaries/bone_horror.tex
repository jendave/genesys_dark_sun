\subsection{Bone Horror}

\Creature[name=Bone Horror, type=Minion, image=images/bone_horror.png, brawn=3, agility=3, intellect=1, cunning=2, willpower=1, presence=1, soak=4, wounds=5, meleedef=1, rangeddef=1, magicdef=0, psionicdef=0]
{
    Bone Horrors are living masses of bone that can reach and move with
    astonishing speed. Only the bravest or most foolish heroes will confront
    these fast and agile constructs head-on.
}{
    Brawl, Atlethics, Coordination, Resilience, Perception\\
}{
    None
}{
    \\ % ability
    \textbf{- Undead:} Does not need to breathe, eat, or drink, and can survive
        underwater; immune to poisons and toxins), Undying (may spend \threat\threat\threat
        from any check made by a PC to return one previously defeated Bone Horror
        to an existing minion group, removing damage from the group accordingly.
        Spend \despair to return two Bone Horrors to a minion group.\\
    \textbf{- Sweep Attack:} A Bone Horror may spend \advantage from a Brawl
        check to hit one additional opponent in enganged or short range that
        would be no more difficult to attack than the original target, dealing
        base damage +1 damage per \success.\\
}{
    \\ % equipment
    \textbf{- Bones: } Brawl; Damage: 5; Critical: 3; Range [Short], \iqtyref{pierce} 1, \iqtyref{knockdown}\\
}
