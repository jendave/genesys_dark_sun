\subsection{Inix}

\Creature[name=Inix, type=Rival, image=images/inix.png, brawn=4, agility=1, intellect=1, willpower=2, presence=1, soak=7, wounds=18, meleedef=0, rangeddef=0, magicdef=0, psionicdef=0]{
Broad and low to the ground, an inix is a reptilian
behemoth. Used widely as a beast of burden, the inix
is capable of incredible feats of strength. It is tough,
plodding, and slow to anger, but once roused, it is
nearly unstoppable.
