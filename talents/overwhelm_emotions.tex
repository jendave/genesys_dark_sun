\newcommand\overwhelmEmotionsTitle{Overwhelm Emotions}
\newcommand\overwhelmEmotionsActive{false}
\newcommand\overwhelmEmotionsRanked{false}
\newcommand\overwhelmEmotionsDescription{ Requires Psionics 1. May add \force per rank in Psionics to Charm, Coerce or Deception checks. }
\newcommand\overwhelmEmotionsTalent{ \Talent[name=\overwhelmEmotionsTitle, active=\overwhelmEmotionsActive, ranked=\overwhelmEmotionsRanked]{
When the character performs a Charm, Coersion or Deception check, she may include \force equal to her ranks in Psionics. Each \light add success to Charm checks. Each \dark adds \success to Coercion or Deception checks. However every \light\light add \threat to Coercion or Deception Checks and every \dark\dark adds \threat to Charm checks. This does not apply to targets with one or more Psionic Defense.
} }
